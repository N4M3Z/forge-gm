Every secret in a scene must have at least two discovery paths. Each path specifies a method, location, and skill check with DC.

```markdown
- **Secret:** [The truth]
  - *Clue A:* [Evidence] — [[Arcana]] DC 15
  - *Clue B:* [Evidence] — [[Investigation]] DC 12
```

A secret with only one clue is a bottleneck. If the player fails that check, the information is lost. Two paths ensure redundancy. Three is better for critical plot information.

Based on The Alexandrian's Three Clue Rule and Sly Flourish's Secrets and Clues technique.
