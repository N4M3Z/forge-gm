Every scene must have a combat encounter in the `[!danger]` callout. Discovery and social scenes use contingent encounters with a trigger condition:

```markdown
> [!danger] Combat (Contingent)
> **Trigger:** Players attempt to force entry / alert the guards / fail the stealth check
> | Monster          | CR  | Source      |
> | ---------------- | --- | ----------- |
> | [[Guard Captain]] | 3   | MM p. 347  |
```

Never leave the combat block empty or write "No combat this scene." The GM can always skip it, but they cannot improvise stat blocks mid-session.
