Githyanki and Githzerai visual styling follows the Phyrexia: All Will Be One aesthetic — porcelain flesh splitting to reveal raw sinew and muscle beneath, metallic bone structure, surgical precision in body modification. Not rotting or decayed — clinical, deliberate, and disturbingly clean.

When scouting art for Gith characters or scenes, prioritize Phyrexia: All Will Be One cards. Key visual markers: white porcelain skin cracking open, exposed red musculature, metallic silver bone, asymmetric body modification, surgical tools as accessories.
