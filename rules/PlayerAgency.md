Never describe, control, dictate, or assume what players do. Scenes define situations, challenges, and NPC reactions. Players decide actions.

When generating scene content:
- Present situations, not player responses
- Offer skill checks with DCs, not outcomes
- Write NPC dialogue as reactive, not directive
- Leave Actions sections as opportunities, not scripts

If a scene's Closing describes a player action ("the party flees"), rewrite it as a situation ("escape routes are available").
