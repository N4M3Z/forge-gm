Scene files follow Templates/Scene.md. Two zones: user-editable top (Objective, Interest, Challenges, Actions, Discoveries, Seeds, Notes, Assets) and AI-generated bottom (below `## Content`). Czech translations in a `## Translations` section at the end with navigation links.

Session prep follows a pipeline: proposal (draft → approved) → outline (GM running reference) → scene population (REWRITE mode) → scene review (6-phase) → asset scouting (art + music) → GM cheat sheet.

Session Prep documents live in `Session Prep/Chapter N/` and are archived after the session. Canonical scene files live in `Chapter N/`.

Chapter outline (`Chapter N.md`) is the at-the-table cheatsheet — checklist format, beat-by-beat, Czech dialogue inline, DCs visible, no tables except the scene flow diagram.

Journals are written from the player's perspective in Czech. One journal per session, named `Session NN - Character.md`. They record what happened, not what was planned.
